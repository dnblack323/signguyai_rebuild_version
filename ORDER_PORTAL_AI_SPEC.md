# Order Portal AI Specification

## AI Principle

AI drafts and recommends. The sign shop reviews, edits, and approves before anything becomes final or public.

## AI Actions

Supported AI actions:

- questionnaire summary
- missing-info checker
- field mapping suggestions
- product suggestions
- product names
- product descriptions
- store description
- fundraiser story
- promotional copy
- launch packet copy
- FAQ generation
- mockup generation
- background cleanup
- image cleanup

## Product Suggestions

AI suggestions may use:

- portal type
- questionnaire answers
- uploaded artwork
- selected known products
- "open to suggestions" flag
- product template library
- default pricing/production cost rules

AI suggestion cards should show:

- suggested product
- source template
- reason
- estimated production cost
- suggested selling price
- suggested owner share
- platform fee estimate
- estimated shop gross before processing
- confidence or warnings where available

Sign shop must approve before suggestions become portal products.

## Artwork And Mockups

- Original artwork is always preserved.
- Cleaned artwork is separate.
- Background removal does not mean production-ready.
- Shop approval controls mockup use and production readiness.
- Owner-visible mockups must be approved by shop first.

## AI Usage Tracking

Track by:

- sign shop
- portal
- AI action
- user
- timestamp
- tokens/credits/cost where available
- result status

AI features may later be plan-limited or sold as credit packs.
